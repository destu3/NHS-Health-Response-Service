About Us

Welcome to the NHS Service Response App! We are a group of medical experts and software developers committed to enhancing patients' access to healthcare in the UK.

Our company's motivation comes from a strong sense of purpose and passion for enhancing the health and wellbeing of people in the UK. We think that technology can significantly contribute to improving the effectiveness, fairness, and patient-centeredness of healthcare.

We appreciate you choosing the NHS Service Response App, and we look forward to continuing helping you in the future.

Our Services

Book Appointments

quickly and easily book appointments with your GP, specialist, or other healthcare providers

Manage Appointments

view and manage your upcoming appointments, receive reminders, and cancel or reschedule if necessary

Discover Health Centers

find NHS health centers near you, including pharmacies, hospitals, and clinic

Request Prescription

request repeat prescriptions for your medications, and have them delivered to your home or a nearby pharmacy

Manage Health Profile

view and update your personal health information, including your medical history, allergies, and medications
